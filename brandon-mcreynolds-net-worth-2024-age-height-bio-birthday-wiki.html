Brandon McReynolds Biography

Brandon McReynolds is one of the most popular and richest Racing Driver who was born on May 21, 1991 in Mooresville, United States. Lawrence Brandon McReynolds (born May 21 1991) is an American professional racer in stock cars and as the official spotter of Noah Gragson. He has participated with JGL Racing in the NASCAR Xfinity Series for JGL Racing as well as participated with the K&N Pro Series East and K&N Pro Series West as in the ARCA Menards Series. His father is the former crew chief and present Fox NASCAR Analyst Larry McReynolds.

McReynolds completed a total of 44 starts, including 29 in the West and 15 in the East between 2011 and the year 2015. McReynolds had the best result of third two times in the East and also took home 2 races of his West series in the year 2015. Both of his wins were based on the pole in Iowa.
