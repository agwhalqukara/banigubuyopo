Who Is Falynn Guobadia of RHOA? Her Age, Wiki &amp; Facts

The thirteenth season of The Real Housewives of Atlanta (RHOA) saw some new additions including an aspiring model and a proud mom, Falynn Guobadia. Falynn surprised everyone by appearing on the show out of the blue on 20 December 2020.

Falynn Guobadia is an American reality TV personality and model who is best recognized for her surprise addition in Bravo’s reality show, The Real Housewives of Atlanta. Besides, she is also known as the better half of the popular businessman.

Falynn Guobadia Wiki; Age, Family, Birthday, Siblings

The 31-years-of-age reality star was born as Falynn Guobadia in 1989, in Florida, the United States. She celebrates her birthday on 13th May every year. Born as Taurus, Falynn comes with mixed ethnicity.


Falynn was born as the second oldest among the 10 children of her parents. Though the actual names of her parents and nine other siblings remain confined, she revealed through her official website that her mother is from Thailand and her father hails from Cape Verdean.

The sensational diva wanted to become a singer when she was young. Her uncle bought her a microphone, speakers, and a karaoke system when she was a little girl so that she could practice singing every day.

Her Career Details; Job, RHOA

Falynn Guobadia may not have worked with countless big brands as a model, but she is definitely a natural in front of the camera. Her Instagram account is full of stunning model shots that just show how photogenic she is.

Besides, Falynn homeschools five boys and is active in the Atlanta social scene. She likes to do collaborations and sponsorships, product giveaways on her social media accounts, and public speaking events.

The fans of RHOA (The Real Housewives of Atlanta) were left surprise and curious when Falynn appeared on the thirteenth season of the show out of complete blue during the Ten Ten, Twenty Twenty episode on 20 December 2020. It is still not clear whether she is regular on the show, which premiered on 8 December 2020.

Falynn Guobadia’s Married Life With Husband Simon Guobadia

As per the reports, Falynn Guobadia shares relationship bliss with an Atlanta-based businessman, Simon Guobadia. The couple walked down the aisle together back in July 2019.

Falynn’s husband, Simon, owned a popular Midtown restaurant in Atlanta which later shut down following the coronavirus pandemic. His previous businesses include a popular Buckhead Bottle Bar and a series of barbershops.

On top of that, Simon is also known for the credit as a producer on multiple films, including Jail Dogs (2013), Ken Ford Live from the Buckhead Theatre (2013), Kill (2019), and Son of the South (2020).

As of now, Falynn and Simon co-parent six children together from their previous relationships. Among them, one is named Troy (age 14), whom Falynn had when she was just 17.

Split With Husband Simon

In April 2021, Falynn announced that she is filing a divorce with her husband Simon.

However, she has not revealed the reason behind their divorce.

Meanwhile, Simon began a new relationship after their separation. But, their divorce is yet to be finalized. He is now engaged o the Real Housewives of Atlanta star Porsha Williams.

  • She purchased Red Light District by Ludacris for herself for her birthday as a teen.
