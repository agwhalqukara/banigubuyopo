Think you love dogs? This man spent $16K to become one

Toco-san is a human that clearly thinks dogs are the best, which we fully agree with. And since us humans can’t beat them at being the best, Toko-san decided to join them and fulfill a life-long dream.

He did this by spending 2 million Yen (about $16,000 USD) and 40 days to have an ultra-realistic collie costume made especially for him.

It was made by Zeppet, a Japanese workshop specializing in custom costumes, modeling, animatronics, and robots, which they usually make for movies and commercials.
