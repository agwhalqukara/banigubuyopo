How many years should a bed last?

A good quality bed can last for as long as you live, depending on how much you use it and how well you care for it. In most cases, however, a mattress will last between six and ten years. Some can last longer, particularly if you sleep alone or have light body weight.

What is the healthiest type of bed to sleep on?

The healthiest mattresses to sleep on are hybrid models. They offer the structural support your complete body requires and layers of quilted cotton and latex comfort on top.
