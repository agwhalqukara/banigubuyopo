Ariana Grande Was at Wimbledon Without Her Wedding Ring, Leaving Fans Concerned: Here’s What We Know

Ariana Grande has been keeping a low profile lately, but she made a rare public appearance at the Wimbledon men’s singles final on Sunday.

The “Wicked” star was photographed in the crowd at the Wimbledon men’s singles final Sunday sans her diamond sparkler on her left finger.

Grande, who was seated between Andrew Garfield and “Bridgerton” star Jonathan Bailey, reacted to moments during the match by covering her mouth with her right hand as her left hand rested on her lap — notably without a ring.

The last time the “God Is a Woman” singer, 30, was seen with her wedding ring in public was back in April at Jeff Goldblum’s concert in London.

The singer’s missing ring raised some eyebrows on social media, as some fans wondered if there was trouble in her marriage.

Others speculated that she might have taken off her ring for safety or comfort reasons, or that she might be getting it cleaned or upgraded.

Here is what we know about Grande’s ring situation and her relationship with Gomez.

Grande has not explained why she did not wear her wedding ring at Wimbledon, but there could be several possible reasons.

One of them is that she might have wanted to keep a low profile and avoid drawing attention to herself.
