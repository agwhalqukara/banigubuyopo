Lou Taylor Pucci (born July 27, 1985) is an American actor who first appeared on film in Rebecca Miller’s Personal Velocity: Three Portraits in 2002. Pucci then starred in The Chumscrubber (2005), Fast Food Nation (2006), The Go-Getter (2007), Explicit Ills (2008), and Carriers (2009). Pucci had starring roles in the 2013 Evil Dead remake, as well as The Story of Luke (2013) and Spring (2014).
