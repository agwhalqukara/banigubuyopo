Chloe Sevigny Disses Jennifer Lawrence as &quot;Crass,&quot; &quot;Annoying&quot;

Apparently not everyone loves J.Law's candor. Jennifer Lawrence has endeared herself to fans with her dry humor and goofy, uninhibited behavior, becoming both a major movie star and an awards show darling. But Chloe Sevigny, for one, is over it.

The Bloodline actress, 40, made her feelings clear in an interview for V Magazine's Spring issue. Asked whether she worries about being typecast, she admitted she's "afraid that maybe people think there's more personality than acting ability."

As writer Natasha Stagg pointed out, however, a big personality is a big part of being a celebrity, at least in the current culture.

"So much is about marketing and selling the product," Sevigny agreed.
