Tega has not come home, I cant even leave my house, husband cries out in new interview (vid

Big Brother Naija ‘Shine Ya Eye’ evicted housemate, Tega’s husband has granted an interview days after his wife got evicted, and he has complaints to make.

Tega happened to be one of the most talked about housemate during her time on the show as she got intimate with another now evicted housemate, Boma despite having a marriage outside.

Tega’s husband, Ajeboh Krislawrence, revealed in an interview with Tunde Ednut that he had been married to Tega for four years and has no idea why his wife did what she did in the house.

Tega had told him before entering the Big Brother House that she would do everything possible in the house except having sex, which he opposed to, instructing her to act like a married lady and represent married people well, according to him. She complied with this request.

Ajeboh went on to say that seeing his wife spending time with another man in the house had surprised and shattered him.

Since his wife’s eviction from the show on Sunday, Ajeboh, who appeared heartbroken and broken, said he hasn’t seen or spoken to her.
