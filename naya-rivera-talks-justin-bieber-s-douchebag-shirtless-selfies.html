Naya Rivera Talks Justin Bieber's &quot;Douchebag&quot; Shirtless Selfies

Not a Belieber! Naya Rivera isn’t a fan of Justin Bieber’s shirtless selfies, and made her disapproval known during The View on Friday, Jan. 16.

“It’s annoying. You look like a douchebag,” the Glee actress, 28, said. “Girls don’t want to date guys that are constantly in a mirror. Like, stop. Go fix something in the house. That’s hotter.”

Co-hosts Rosie O’Donnell and Nicolle Wallace couldn’t help but agree. “Listen, I went over this yesterday. I think he’s a tool,” Wallace said. “I don’t think he can fix anything in the house, and I think he spends way too much. He probably has that camera that lets you take your own selfie.”

Referring to one specific photo, Rivera added: “Obviously he showers a lot because he had the towel around his waist. He just got out of the shower.”
