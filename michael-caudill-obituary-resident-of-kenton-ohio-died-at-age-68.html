Obituary for Michael Caudill

Remembering the Life and Legacy of Michael Caudill

It is with heavy hearts that we bid farewell to our beloved Michael Caudill, a cherished member of the Kenton, Ohio community. On December 23, 2023, Michael passed away at the age of 68, leaving behind a profound impact on the lives of those who knew him. Today, we gather to honor his memory and celebrate a life well-lived.

Condolences and Guestbook

Expressing Your Sympathies to the Caudill Family

In this time of grief, we invite you to share your condolences and memories of Michael in the guestbook provided. Your kind words and support will bring comfort to his family as they navigate through this difficult period. Your presence, even in the virtual space, will serve as a reminder of the love and support that surrounds them.

Predeceased Family Members

Remembering Those Who Went Before

Michael Caudill was preceded in death by his siblings, Carl Caudill Jr., John Banford, and Cheryl Durkin. He also mourned the loss of his parents, Carl Caudill and Dorothy Caudill (Webb), as well as his stepmother, Peggy Caudill. Though they are no longer with us, their memory lives on in the hearts of those who loved them.

Surviving Family Members

A Legacy of Love and Connection

Michael is survived by his children, Mende Klesmit and Michael Caudill, who will carry on his legacy of love and strength. He is also survived by his siblings, Gary Caudill, David Caudill, Timothy Caudill, Brian Caudill, Lori Ann Eversole, Karla Edwards, Tim Durkin, Perry Bond, and Roger Durkin. Additionally, he leaves behind his grandchildren, Tyler Caudill, Dylan Hale (Riley McKinnis), and Abby Rans, as well as four step-grandchildren and a large number of nieces and nephews. The bonds of family and the memories they shared will forever hold a special place in their hearts.

Visitation and Funeral Details

Honoring Michael’s Life and Saying Goodbye

Visitation will be held at the Price McElroy Funeral Home on Thursday, December 28th, 2023, from 11:00 AM to 1:00 PM. The funeral home, located at 123 N Main St, Kenton, Ohio 43326, will be a place for friends and family to gather, pay their respects, and share their memories of Michael. Following the visitation, a funeral service will be held at the same location at one o’clock in the afternoon. Let us come together to celebrate a life that touched so many and find solace in the shared memories we hold dear.

Memorial Contributions

Honoring Michael’s Legacy through Giving

In memory of Michael Caudill, memorial contributions can be made to the St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital. By supporting this noble cause, we can continue Michael’s spirit of compassion and make a difference in the lives of children in need. Your generosity will honor his memory and bring hope to those who need it most.
