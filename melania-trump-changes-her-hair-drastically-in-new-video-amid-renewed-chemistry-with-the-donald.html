Melania Trump Changes Her Hair Drastically In New Video Amid Renewed Chemistry With The Donald

First Lady Melania Trump has decided to drastically change her looks for the Christmas holiday, and the move will probably keep the steamy chemistry that exists between her and The Donald alive.

This week, Melania with a very light blonde hair color sat down for a penetrating interview with Sean Hannity on the Fox News Channel where she spoke about falling in love with the businessman back in 1998.

She said: “We had a great chemistry when we met in 1998 and we started dating, and we dated for a long time before we got married, and we got married, we had a son, but we had a great chemistry from the day one.”

The former model went on to explain: “It’s incredible, especially when you think about your life, where you were born and all the steps you took. I took a lot of steps; it’s not just like you end up here.”

The mother of one also took the opportunity to slam her critics by saying: “You learn a lot on the way here and in my business, in fashion business, it’s a tough business as well and here the politics as well, it’s a tough business, and you need to have very thick skin.”

A source told Hollywood Life that the holidays bring out the flirty and loving side of Melania.

The insider claimed: “Melania is always in a way better frame of mind when it’s the Holidays, and she automatically feels more loving and warm towards Donald, as she does the rest of her family too at this time of the year. Melania is a huge fan of the Holidays, especially Christmas, and ever since she was a little girl it has always been an important and big deal for her, and that’s only intensified since becoming First Lady.”
