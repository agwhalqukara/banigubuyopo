Gina Gershon

Birthday

June 10, 1962 (61)

Gina Gershon is an American actress, singer, and author, recognized for her performances in “Showgirls” (1995), “Face/Off” (1997), and “Bound” (1996). Her career encompasses roles in film, television, and stage, contributing to a variety of genres including drama, comedy, and action.

In film, Gershon’s portrayal in “Showgirls” and “Bound” established her presence in the industry, with “Bound” particularly noted for its critical reception. Her involvement in “Face/Off” added to her portfolio of action films. On television, Gershon has appeared in “Curb Your Enthusiasm,” “Rescue Me,” and “Riverdale,” demonstrating her adaptability across different formats. Her voice acting contributions include work on “Tripping the Rift,” expanding her range into animated entertainment.

Gershon’s career has been marked by collaborations with notable industry figures, including directors Paul Verhoeven (“Showgirls”) and the Wachowskis (“Bound”), which contributed to her early recognition. Her role in “Face/Off” involved collaboration with actors John Travolta and Nicolas Cage under the direction of John Woo. On television, her appearance in “Curb Your Enthusiasm” saw her working with creator and star Larry David, while her participation in “Riverdale” as Gladys Jones introduced her to a new audience.
